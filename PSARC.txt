File Format
.m2v - Basic dvd video/video compression
.vpk - Voice pack (english) just .wav format but renamed to .vpk
.vp1 - Voice pack 1 (idk) same as .vpk
.vp2 - Voice pack 2 (idk) same as .vpk
.vp3 - Voice pack 3 (idk) same as .vpk
.vp4 - Voice pack 4 (idk) same as .vpk
.wad_ps3 - 3d model-maps-game logic - you can use God of war browser
.vag - just sfx in the game(ex: npc get hurt) maybe same like .vpk (i didnt test it yet)
